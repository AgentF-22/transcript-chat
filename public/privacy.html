Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Noted ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information we collect

When you use Noted, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address, collected when you sign in via email or Google.
  • Transcript content — the transcripts you upload are processed to answer your questions. They are not stored on our servers — they exist only in your browser session.
  • Usage data — we track how many messages you send per day to enforce plan limits.
  • Support messages — messages you send via our support system are stored so we can respond to you.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the Noted service
  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To enforce usage limits based on your plan
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data storage and security

Your account data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Authentication is handled by Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption. Transcripts you upload are never saved to our database — they are processed in memory and discarded when your session ends.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and user management
  • Railway — cloud hosting
  • Groq — AI inference for answering your questions
  • Google — optional sign-in via Google OAuth

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at khzsum2019@gmail.com.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed within 30 days. Support message history is retained for 12 months.

7. Cookies

Noted uses minimal cookies — only those required for authentication (session tokens). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at khzsum2019@gmail.com.
